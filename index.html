About Me

Gina R. Bai

I am a 4th year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at North Carolina State University where I am advised by Dr. Kathryn T. Stolee in the Collaborative Software Engineering Lab. My research interests are in the areas of testing, code search, empirical software engineering, and CS education.

Please reach out to me at rbai2@ncsu.edu if you would like to collaborate or talk more about my research!
